About Us

We are a full service heating and air conditioning system repair and installation company in California. We are licensed and insured for residential as well as commercial work. Our goal is to provide indoor comfort for home and businesses all year long. EZ is well known for its top-notch quality HVAC services and products. In addition, we offer 24-hour a day and 7-days per week emergency repair services as well as same-day services.

Do you want your cooling or heating system fixed? EZ specializes in all aspects of the heat and air systems solutions such as installation, repair, and maintenance services. We have an expert team of professionals who ensure superb workmanship, as they have decades of experience. We provide exceptional customer service.

We strive to deliver reliable and durable products that meet our customers' expectations. We only hire licensed, highly qualified and trained technicians to serve you. We have built a great reputation, thanks to our customer's appreciation and feedback on the quality service we offer.
