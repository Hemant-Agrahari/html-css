FAQs

Multi-zone mini-split systems can support up to 8 separate indoor units from a single outdoor condenser, providing individual climate control for almost any sized home.

Mini-splits are often more efficient because they avoid energy loss associated with ductwork. They also allow for zoned cooling, which is ideal if you only want to cool specific parts of your home.

Most single-zone installations can be completed in a single day. Multi-zone systems may take 2-3 days depending on the complexity of the line sets and indoor unit mounting.
