Ideal Ways To Clean The Filters Of Your Air Conditioner

Clean The Indoor Unit:

The indoor air conditioner unit contains dust filters that need regular cleaning. The more clogged the filter will be, the harder it will be for them to push the cool air. You only need to pop up the plastic cover and remove the filter to clean this. If you cannot reach it, try to use a small stepladder.

The manufacturers always mentioned cleaning the ac filters within a few weeks. But wait, certain factors signify how consecutively you should use this. The more you use the air conditioner, the more you should clean the filters. If you use it in minimal time, even then, you should clean it at least once a year.

Some models in the market come with an air purification filter. This type of filter is easy to remove and maintain. Take a brush and shake it on the filter to remove all the dust. If it is in the worst condition, use warm water with some mild detergent and then rinse them. Once it dries, properly install them in their position.
