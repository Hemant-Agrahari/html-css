Seer Scores The Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio (SEER) is one of the most essential elements in identifying the efficiency of your HVAC systems. No matter which AC unit you prefer for your home or office, SEER ratings are there in every unit ranging from 13 to 28. The federal government mandates air conditioners to have a SEER rating of at least 13 (poor efficiency) to all the way up to 28 (high efficiency).
